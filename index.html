About Me

Historically worked in industrial and medical device manufacturing in Financial Manager, Corporate Controller, and Director of Operations roles.


Most recently led all aspects of business administration for a building materials manufacturer with $65,000,000 in annual sales and 430 employees. Completed years' long process of preparing the company for acquisition through transformation of financial reporting products and timelines, human resource management, governmental compliance, and administrative functionality.


Now working with small and mid-size business that are growing, streamlining, or struggling. Adding value and reducing costs across business functions, while giving management increased visibility to financial and supply chain information.
